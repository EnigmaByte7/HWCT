Welcome to Shelter of love

Shelter of Love is a home for the underprivileged orphan , semi - orphan children between the age group pf 6-18 yrs .Started as an orphanage now supports the local communities mainly children , youth and women to upgrade their skills through programs proposed by HWCT for the skill centre. The shelter is also equipped to expand its facilities to support other support programs like extending Medical Camps and aiding people with different abilities in various aspects .
